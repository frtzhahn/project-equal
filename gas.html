What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 General Academic Strand?

GAS or also known as General Academic Strand have at least 4 Specialized subject for the for the 1st Semester and 3 Specialized subject in 2nd Semester in which a total of 7 Specialized Subjects for the whole semester of Grade 12 GAS

And here are the following Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 GAS which is divided into 2 semesters:

    FIRST SEMESTER

  • Disaster Readiness and Risk Reduction
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Business Mathematics
  • Creative Writing

    SECOND SEMESTER

  • Creative Non-fiction
  • Applied Economics
  • Work Immersion
