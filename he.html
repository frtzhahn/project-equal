What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 HE?

HE or also known as Home Economics have at least 1 Specialized subject for the for the 1st Semester and also 1 Specialized subject in 2nd Semester, in which a total of 2 Specialized Subjects for the whole semester of Grade 12 HE

And here are the following Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 HE which is divided into 2 semesters:

    FIRST SEMESTER

  • Cookery NC II

    SECOND SEMESTER

  • Bread & Pastry Production
