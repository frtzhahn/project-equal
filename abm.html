What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 ABM strand?

ABM or also known as Accountancy and Business Management strand have at least 3 Specialized subject for the for the 1st Semester and 4 Specialized subject in 2nd Semester in which a total of 7 Specialized Subjects for the whole semester of Grade 12 ABM

And here are the following Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 ABM Strand which is divided into 2 semesters:

    FIRST SEMESTER

  • Fundamentals of Accountancy,
    Business and Management (FABM 2)
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Business Mathematics

    SECOND SEMESTER

  • Business Ethics and Social Responsibility
  • Business Enterprise Simulation
  • Business Finance
  • Applied Economics
