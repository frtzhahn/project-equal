What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 HUMSS?

HUMSS or also known as Humanities & Social Sciences have at least 4 Specialized subjects for the for the 1st Semester and 1 Specialized subject in 2nd Semester in which a total of 5 Specialized Subjects for the whole semester of Grade 12 HUMSS

And here are the following Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 HUMSS which is divided into 2 semesters:

    FIRST SEMESTER

  • Community Engagement, Solidarity & Citizenship
  • Trends, Networks & Critical Thinking in the 21st Century
  • Disciplines & Ideas in the Applied Social Sciences
  • Creative Writing

    SECOND SEMESTER

  • Creative Non-Fiction
