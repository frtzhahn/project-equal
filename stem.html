What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 STEM?

STEM or also known as Science Technology Engineering & Mathematics have at least 2 Specialized subjects for the for the 1st Semester and 3 Specialized subjects in 2nd Semester in which a total of 5 Specialized Subjects for the whole semester of Grade 12 STEM

And here are the following Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 STEM which is divided into 2 semesters:

    FIRST SEMESTER

  • General Physics 1
  • General Chemestry 2

    SECOND SEMESTER

  • General Physics 2
